The Artists Behind the Chair

Ron

Even though Ron is new to the barbering scene, he's quickly made a name for himself with his precise cuts and keen attention to detail. Specializing in classic fades and modern hairstyles, Ron has a knack for delivering sharp, clean looks tailored to each client’s style. His passion for learning and perfecting his craft ensures that every cut leaves you looking your best.

Tony

Tony is a true master when it comes to creative designs and expert scissor work. With years of experience, he brings an artistic touch to every cut, ensuring that each customer leaves with a unique, custom look that suits their personality and style. Whether it’s a bold design or a perfectly crafted classic cut, Tony’s skill and creativity make him a standout barber.

Jet

Jet is a passionate barber who feels blessed to be in his profession. A self-proclaimed nerd who loves video games and movies, he believes barbering and connecting with clients are his top priorities. His firm belief in following the right path guides him in both life and work.
